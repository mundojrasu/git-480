Remodels

Residential additions are a great way to add value to your home.

Our design process is broken into four design phases:

  1. Existing Conditions: Information gathering and documentation of the site and existing home.
  2. Schematic Design: Schematic designs integrate the desired design into the home's overall concept.
  3. Design Development: Design Development is where the home and its addition com together.
  4. Construction Documents: Construction Documents are the final set of deliverables.
